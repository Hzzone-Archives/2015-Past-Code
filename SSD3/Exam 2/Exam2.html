Exam 2

This exam has two timed components, one multiple-choice and one practical. You must complete both of these components in the time allotted to each to be eligible for full credit. However, you may do the components in any order. Each is timed separately, so they can be completed at different times.

Student performance from past semesters indicates that success in this exam is highly correlated with success in the exercises and practical-quizzes that come before the exam. You are strongly encouraged to master all the exercises and practical-quizzes prior to taking the exam.
